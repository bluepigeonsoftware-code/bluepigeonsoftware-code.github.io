SuperMag Privacy Policy

Blue Pigeon Software

The app uses Android screen capture and overlay features to enlarge content on your device. Screen content is processed locally on-device for magnification. We do not sell your data. We do not upload or store your screen content on our own servers.

The free version of the app uses Google AdMob to show ads. Google and its partners may collect data needed to provide and measure ads, in accordance with Google’s privacy policy. If you purchase Pro, the payment is processed by Google Play Billing. We do not store your payment information.

The app may store limited information locally on your device, but this information is never uploaded to our servers.

Permissions used:

  • Display over other apps: to show the magnifier lens
  • Foreground service / media projection: to magnify screen content
  • Internet / network access: for ads and purchases
