EverCalc Privacy Policy

Blue Pigeon Software
Last updated: May 4, 2026

EverCalc is a calculator app with a floating-window mode and optional monetization features. Calculations, calculation history, app settings, and floating-window content are processed locally on your device. Blue Pigeon Software does not upload this content to its own servers, does not sell your data, and does not use your calculator activity for tracking.

Information the app stores locally

EverCalc may store limited app data on your device, such as settings, calculation state, history, purchase entitlement state, and other preferences needed for the app to work. This local data is not uploaded to Blue Pigeon Software servers.

Third-party services

The free version of EverCalc may use Google AdMob to show ads. Google and its advertising partners may collect data needed to provide, personalize, limit, and measure ads, such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, app activity related to ads, and approximate location inferred by Google. This data is handled by Google under Google's policies: Google Privacy Policy and Google Advertising Policy.

If crash or error reporting is enabled, EverCalc may send diagnostic information to Sentry to help identify and fix crashes or bugs. This may include device and operating system information, app version, timestamps, crash logs, stack traces, and technical details about the error. Sentry handles this information under its policy: Sentry Privacy Policy.

If you purchase a paid feature, the purchase is processed by Google Play Billing. Blue Pigeon Software does not receive or store your payment card information.

Permissions used

  • Display over other apps: to show the floating calculator window.
  • Internet and network access: for ads, purchases, and diagnostic reporting.
  • Vibration: to provide haptic feedback for calculator interactions.

Children

EverCalc is not directed to children under 13. Blue Pigeon Software does not knowingly collect personal information from children through EverCalc.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when EverCalc changes or when third-party services change their privacy practices. Updates will be posted on this page.
