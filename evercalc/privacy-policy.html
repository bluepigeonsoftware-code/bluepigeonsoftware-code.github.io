EverCalc Privacy Policy

Blue Pigeon Software
Last updated: May 4, 2026

EverCalc keeps calculations, history, settings, and floating-window content on your device. Blue Pigeon Software does not upload this data to its own servers, sell it, or use it for tracking.

The app may use Google AdMob for ads. Google and its partners may collect data needed to show and measure ads, such as device identifiers, advertising ID, IP address, app/device information, and ad interactions. Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing; we do not store payment card information.

If Sentry crash reporting is enabled, Sentry may receive diagnostics such as app version, device/OS information, timestamps, stack traces, and error logs to help fix bugs.

Permissions used:

  • Display over other apps: to show the floating calculator
  • Internet / network access: for ads, purchases, and diagnostics
  • Vibration: for haptic feedback

EverCalc is suitable for ages 8 and above. We do not knowingly collect children's personal information. Policy updates will be posted on this page.
